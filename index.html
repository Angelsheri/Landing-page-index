FAQ

Answers, before you have to ask.

What kinds of game servers can I run?

Any modern game server that runs on Linux — Minecraft, Rust, Valheim, ARK, CS2, Palworld, and dozens more. Dedicated CPU and NVMe keep tick rates stable even at full player capacity.

How fast is the hosting hardware?

Every plan runs on AMD EPYC processors with NVMe SSDs and 10 Gbps networking. CPU allocation is guaranteed — never oversold, never throttled.

What uptime can I expect?

We back a 99.9% uptime SLA contractually. If we fall short of that in any given month, you receive account credits automatically, no ticket required.

How does deployment work?

Connect a GitHub repository, set environment variables, and push. Stratum builds the image, runs health checks, and rolls it out with zero downtime. Rollback is a single click.

Can I scale up or down instantly?

Yes. Resources adjust in seconds without migrating servers, reconfiguring services, or contacting anyone. Scale to match traffic and pay for exactly what you use.
