FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hosting your bots, game servers, and apps on Stratum.

Can I host my Discord bot on Stratum?

Yes. Stratum is built for Discord bots in Node.js, Python, Java, Go, and more. Connect your repo, add your bot token as an environment variable, and deploy. Your bot stays online 24/7 with automatic restarts and instant scaling.

Does Stratum support Telegram bot hosting?

Yes. Both long polling and webhooks are supported, with free HTTPS endpoints and the same low-latency network as the rest of Stratum.

What kinds of game servers can I run?

Minecraft, Rust, Valheim, CS2, and custom builds. Dedicated CPU and NVMe storage keep tick rates stable, while DDoS protection and one-click restarts help keep everything available.

How fast is the hosting hardware?

Stratum uses dedicated AMD EPYC processors, NVMe SSD storage, and ECC memory — never shared. The infrastructure is designed to deliver consistent performance under load.

What uptime can I expect?

A contractual 99.9% uptime SLA, backed by redundant infrastructure, health checks, and automatic failover when something goes wrong.

How does deployment work?

Connect GitHub. Every push builds, containerizes, and rolls out live with zero downtime. Logs stream live, rollbacks are simple, and your deployment history stays mapped to your Git history.
